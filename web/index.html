Urban mobility · Travel-demand modeling

Model how a city moves.

Project ACTS turns a coded travel survey into a chain of discrete-choice models — then animates agents, desire lines, and route heatmaps on an interactive map. A free desktop tool for transportation engineers and planners.

⬇ Download for Windows View source

Free and open source · Sample dataset: Quezon City, Philippines

Built for travel-demand analysis

Everything the modeling workflow needs, in one desktop app.

🧭

Discrete-choice models

Multinomial & binary logit estimation with automatic significance filtering and collinearity checks.

🚶

Agent animation

Watch simulated travellers move across the road network in real time, filtered by socio-demographic attributes.

🕸️

Desire lines & OD flows

Visualize origin–destination demand directly from your survey and OD matrix.

🔥

Route heatmaps

See where trips concentrate on the network with routed, weighted heatmaps.

📄

CSV in, results out

Upload survey, OD, and choice-set CSVs; export model results and summaries to spreadsheets.

💻

Runs on your desktop

The visualization runs locally — your data stays on your machine. No account required.

The modeling pipeline

ACTS estimates a behavioural chain of choices, one model feeding the next.

Travel decision → Activity choice → Destination choice → Mode choice

Get started

Download the desktop app and load the bundled Quezon City sample, or bring your own coded survey.
